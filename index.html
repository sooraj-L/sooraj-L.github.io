About
The story so far

My path into data analytics didn't start in a classroom — it started on the floor of a fast-paced restaurant operation.

As a Second Assistant Manager at McDonald's Canada, I worked with operational data every day — tracking sales, waste, and inventory metrics in Excel, monitoring KPIs, and making decisions that affected both people and profit. I didn't have the title of analyst, but I was always asking the same question: what does the data actually tell us?

That curiosity became a commitment. Over the past year I've been building the technical foundation to match it — learning Python, SQL, Power BI, and Excel through hands-on projects, including a Hospital Readmission Risk Analysis and a Retail Sales Dashboard.

I also hold graduate certificates in Business Analytics and Project Management from Cambrian College, Ontario — giving me both the analytical framework and the stakeholder communication skills to complement my self-taught technical stack.
